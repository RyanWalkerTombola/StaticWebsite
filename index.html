2001 An idea was Born

In 2001, Elon Musk conceptualized the Mars Oasis project, he had plans to take an experimental greenhouse to Mars in an attempt to regain public interest in space exploration. "This would be the furthest that life's ever traveled" Musk tried to buy cheap rockets from Russia but couldn't find any for an affordable price. On the flight home, Musk realised he could start a company that could build the affordable rockets he needed. Musk calculated that the raw material cost of a conventional rocket was only 3% of the sale price, by applying vertical intergration, producing 85% of launch hardware in-house, and the modual approach from software engineering, he could cut the launch price by a factor of 10 and still enjoy a 70% gross margin.
